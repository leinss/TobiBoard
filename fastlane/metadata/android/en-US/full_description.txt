TobiBoard is a privacy-first Android keyboard, forked from HeliBoard (which is based on AOSP / OpenBoard). Core typing runs locally on-device. Two optional AI features — Voice-to-Text and Text Fix — only run when you turn them on and supply your own API key.

No accounts. No subscription. No telemetry. No backend. Bring your own key.

<b>AI Voice-to-Text</b>
Long-press Return, tap the mic, speak. Your words land as polished text.
<ul>
  <li>Choose OpenRouter audio models or PayPerQ speech models</li>
  <li>Add a custom prompt or vocabulary so it nails names and jargon</li>
  <li>Local recordings are deleted the moment they're sent</li>
</ul>

<b>AI Text Fix</b>
Select rough text, long-press Return, hit Fix. Get back a clean message in the same language.
<ul>
  <li>Works in any language your model supports</li>
  <li>Cleans typos, awkward phrasing, or shifts tone (formal ↔ casual)</li>
  <li>You review the rewrite before replacing the original</li>
</ul>

<b>Zero Data Retention</b>
TobiBoard requests OpenRouter zero-data-retention endpoints by default. When the model offers a ZDR route, your audio and text aren't logged, stored, or used for training. API keys are encrypted with the Android Keystore, excluded from cloud backups, and never written to logs.

<b>All the HeliBoard features you expect</b>
<ul>
  <li>Add dictionaries for suggestions and spell check</li>
  <li>Customize themes (style, colors, background image); follows system day/night and dynamic colors</li>
  <li>Customize keyboard layouts and special layouts (symbols, number, functional keys)</li>
  <li>Multilingual typing</li>
  <li>Clipboard history</li>
  <li>One-handed mode and split keyboard</li>
  <li>Number pad</li>
  <li>Backup and restore your settings and custom assets</li>
</ul>

<b>Privacy footnote</b>
Once your audio or selected text reaches OpenRouter, PayPerQ, or the underlying model provider, their policies apply. Read their privacy terms before pointing this at anything sensitive.

TobiBoard installs side-by-side with HeliBoard, so you can keep both.
